Nine Original Operators

After years of calamities and endless wars, humankind was on the edge of extinction. Then, a spaceship was spotted floating adrift near the Moon. Nine sleeping machines were found inside and they brought the test of time with them… New and powerful technology was discovered within their cores. They were called the BLIGHT:SURVIVALS .

All the possible Operators will be formed using the parts from the nine originals, mixing them together. These nine types have been categorized as Stronghold, Renovator, Codebreaker, Deathbringer, Groundsmasher, Sumrak, Netshadow, Scrapmaster and Morphex. Find out about each of them to know their characteristics!

Meaning billions of combinations

The possibilities are practically endless! As Operators are created from random combinations of different parts, being nine original Operators with a total of six unique parts each one, it will cause every result into something unique. But these Operators are more than their assembled parts, as they also have a core and internal genes that will have and impact on the stats when they’re built.

With a team of fixers to take care of all

Fixers are well known across Blight City, as they’re the most skilled and talented professionals regarding building and maintenance. They will assist you in the workshop to keep your squad nice and ready for the fight. Fair warning, though! While their prowess is top class, that has nothing to do with their attitudes, so choose wisely the ones who fit you better!
